Explore the magical desert in Marrakech!

Just one hour outside Marrakech you'll find the beautiful Moroccan desert Agafay. There you can enjoy a camel ride during the golden hours or a more active adventure like quad biking across the sand dunes.

Take a stroll through the souks

Wander through the vivid souk markets at Jemaa el-Fnaa filled with the morccans traditional kaftans, the colorful spices and their beautiful handmade crafts.

The culinary experiance of traditional moroccan cuisine

Close your eyes and let the rich flavors of Moroccan cuisine take your taste buds on a trip of a lifetime. It's a must to try the slow-cooked tagines or the traditional couscous, all filled with spices, herbs and local ingredients.
