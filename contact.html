Travel basics for visiting Marrakech

  • Best time to visit: Spring and autumn(don't forget your sunblock).
  • Travel with cash as paying with a card is not always possible, specially when shopping in markets and taking a taxi.
  • The price is always neogotiable so try to bargin before agreeing to pay.
